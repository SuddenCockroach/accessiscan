Welcome to AccessiScan

AccessiScan makes your website welcoming for everyone by checking it against WCAG 2.1 standards. It’s built for small businesses and developers to spot and fix accessibility issues easily.

Most websites (over 96%) have accessibility gaps, which can exclude people or lead to legal issues. Our quick scans (10-30 seconds) and simple tips help you create an inclusive site that works for all, including those with visual or hearing needs.

Enjoy a friendly interface with fun features like scan streaks and confetti for perfect scores. Start scanning now to make your website a better place for everyone!
